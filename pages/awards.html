Hall of Fame

2025 WINNER

AttendAI: Redefining Campus Presence

AttendAI is a cutting-edge, AI-powered university attendance system designed to eliminate the friction of manual registers. By utilizing real-time facial recognition, the system automatically tracks student presence the moment they enter a lecture hall.

Beyond simple tracking, professors are provided with a live dashboard featuring deep-dive analytics on attendance rates, weekly engagement trends, and automatic "at-risk" alerts for students falling behind. This project stood out for its seamless integration of complex computer vision with a highly intuitive administrative interface.

2024 WINNER

BuildScope: Precision Estimating

BuildScope revolutionized the pre-development phase with its AI-driven web project estimator. By analyzing complex project requirements, it instantly generates highly accurate cost breakdowns and realistic development timelines that factor in historical data.

The platform's standout feature is its "Scope-Creep Detector," which flags potential third-party integration complexities and provides a confidence score. This ensures that both clients and developers are fully aligned and aware of technical risks before a single line of production code is written. It is the ultimate tool for transparent project planning.
